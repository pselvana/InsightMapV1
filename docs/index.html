About InsightMap

InsightMap is a free, open-source educational tool designed for university students. Built with React, D3.js, and spaCy, it transforms your notes into insightful visual maps in seconds, helping you identify and explore the relationships between key concepts.

Future Roadmap

  • Import notes from PDFs, Google Docs, and Notion
  • Semantic clustering and filtering by concept type
  • 3D and multi-layered visualizations
  • Community-shared concept maps
